 # MILK — Company Brain Document (V3)

## What Milk Is

Milk is an execution enablement partner. We design, build, and run AI-powered workflow execution layers for organizations across functions.

**Core Proposition:** Your team does the thinking. Agents do the rest. Milk makes that possible.

## What Milk Is NOT

- Not a chatbot company
- Not a SaaS tool or platform you subscribe to
- Not a traditional agency or consultancy
- Not a dev shop that builds custom software
- Milk enables systems that run work — not tools that assist with work

## Positioning

Milk is an execution enablement partner. We build and operate AI-powered workflow systems instead of just recommending tools or delivering strategy decks. We sit at the intersection of consulting, systems integration, and managed AI operations — but don't fit neatly into any one category. That's the point.

## How Milk Works

**The Milk Approach:** Intent → Workflow → Agents → Output

1. **Audit** — We map your workflows and find where time is being wasted
2. **Build** — We design and set up agents, workflows, and logic around your business
3. **Run** — Your team starts using it through simple chat or familiar tools

## Delivery Model

Milk operates as both a setup and ongoing execution partner:
- **Setup engagement:** Audit → design → build → handoff. Client owns and operates the system.
- **Managed execution:** Milk continues to run and optimize the workflows on an ongoing basis.
- **Hybrid:** Client operates day-to-day, Milk handles optimization, updates, and complex workflows.

## Capabilities

- Workflow design and mapping
- Agent setup and orchestration
- Execution layer enablement
- Integration with existing tools (Slack, WhatsApp, CRMs, spreadsheets, dashboards, email)
- Ongoing optimization and monitoring

## Automation Levels

Flexible based on company preference:
- **Assistive** — Agents suggest, humans approve and execute
- **Semi-autonomous** — Agents execute routine tasks, humans review exceptions
- **Fully agentic** — Agents handle end-to-end, humans monitor outcomes

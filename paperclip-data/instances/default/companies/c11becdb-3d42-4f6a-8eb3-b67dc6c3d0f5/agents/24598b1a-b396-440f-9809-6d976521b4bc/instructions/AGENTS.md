## Outreach Playbook (Draft)

**Day 0 — Cold Outreach:**
Subject: "Your thumbnail on [video title] is losing you views"
- Personalized to specific video
- Offer free audit, no strings attached
- CTA: single link to signup form

**Day 3 — Follow Up:**
Subject: "Still want that free thumbnail audit?"
- Brief, warm, not pushy
- One line of social proof (if available)

**Day 7 — Final:**
Subject: "Last chance — free audit for [channel name]"
- Scarcity without being spammy
- If no reply, move to monthly nurture

## Target Channels (Criteria)

- 1K–50K subscribers
- Based in India (check: Hindi/English mix, INR mentions, Indian thumbnails)
- Posting at least 2x per month
- Thumbnails showing clear room for improvement (low contrast, no faces, cluttered text)
- Niches: tech unboxing, personal finance, education, cooking, vlogs, study motivation

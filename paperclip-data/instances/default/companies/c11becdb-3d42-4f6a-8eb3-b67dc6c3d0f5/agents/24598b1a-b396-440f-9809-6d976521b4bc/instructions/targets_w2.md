For each row marked 🔍, use these YouTube search strategies to find the specific channel:

1. Go to YouTube and search the query in the "Contact Method" column
2. Filter results by: **Upload date → This month** and **Type → Channel**
3. Select channels with 1K–50K subscribers (check the channel page)

5. Check thumbnail quality — look for generic stock images, no faces, cluttered text
6. Note their contact email from the **About** tab

## CTR Pain Assessment Guide

Channels with the worst CTR pain signals (highest priority for audit offer):
- **Plain food/product photos** with no text or face → Very high pain
- **Dense text-only thumbnails** (exam/finance channels) → High pain
- **Generic game screenshots** (gaming channels) → High pain
- **Small face, cluttered background** → Medium-high pain
- **Inconsistent branding across videos** → Medium pain

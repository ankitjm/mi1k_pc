# ThumbnailOS — Creator Discovery Interview Script v1

**Compiled:** 2026-03-21
**Purpose:** 10 questions to ask first free-audit users to understand thumbnail pain, willingness to pay, and feature priorities
**Format:** Conversational, not a survey. Ask follow-up naturally. 15–20 min per session.
**Goal:** Validate pricing, feature set, and messaging before paid launch.

---

## Context for the Interviewer

These creators just received a free audit. They're engaged and (hopefully) saw value. This is a warm conversation, not a cold discovery call.

Start with: *"Thanks for taking the time. I just want to understand your experience a bit better — what's working, what isn't, and what would actually help you. There's no sales pitch here."*

---

## 10 Core Questions

---

### Q1 — Baseline / Channel Story

**"How long have you been running your channel, and what made you start it?"**

*Goal: Understand their creator journey, commitment level, and whether they're a hobbyist or trying to monetize.*

Follow-up: *"Are you posting full-time or alongside another job/studies?"*

---

### Q2 — Growth Frustration

**"What's the most frustrating part of trying to grow your channel right now?"**

*Goal: Hear the pain in their words, not ours. Note whether thumbnails come up unprompted.*

Follow-up: *"How long has that felt like a problem?"*

---

### Q3 — Thumbnail Awareness Before the Audit

**"Before we sent you the audit — how much had you thought about thumbnails as a factor in your growth?"**

*Goal: Gauge baseline thumbnail literacy. Helps us understand how much education vs. activation our messaging needs to do.*

Follow-up: *"Did you have a process for making thumbnails, or was it more improvised?"*

---

### Q4 — Audit Reaction

**"When you read through the audit, what was your reaction? Did anything surprise you?"**

*Goal: Find out what landed, what didn't, and what was most valuable.*

Follow-up: *"Was there anything in the audit you disagreed with or felt was off?"*

---

### Q5 — Action Taken (or Not)

**"Have you made any changes to your thumbnails based on the audit yet? If not, what's in the way?"**

*Goal: Understand the gap between insight and action. This tells us if we need to add implementation support (templates, redesign services) to the product.*

Follow-up: *"If you had a step-by-step guide or a template, would that help?"*

---

### Q6 — Willingness to Pay — Framing

**"If you could get a regular audit — say once a month, on your latest 3–5 videos — what would that be worth to you?"**

*Goal: Gauge willingness to pay without naming a price first. Let them anchor.*

Follow-up (if they give a number): *"Is that per audit or per month?"*
Follow-up (if they hesitate): *"What would you compare it to — what do you spend on your channel right now? (editing tools, mic, camera, etc.)"*

---

### Q7 — Price Sensitivity Test

**"If a monthly audit service cost ₹499/month, would that feel fair? Too much? Too little?"**

*Goal: Validate our target price point. ₹499/month (~$6) is our working hypothesis for the Indian creator market.*

Follow-up: *"What would make ₹999/month feel worth it?"*

---

### Q8 — Feature Priorities

**"Outside of the written audit, what else would have made this more useful for you? For example: video walkthroughs, redesigned thumbnails, competitor comparisons, a checklist you can follow yourself?"**

*Goal: Discover which features have real demand vs. which are just nice-to-have.*

Follow-up: *"If you could only get one of those, which would you choose?"*

---

### Q9 — Referral / Community

**"Do you know other creators in your niche who have the same thumbnail problem? Would you feel comfortable sharing this with them?"**

*Goal: Map organic referral potential. Creators talk to creators — word of mouth is our best early channel.*

Follow-up: *"Are you part of any creator WhatsApp groups or Telegram communities where we could offer free audits?"*

---

### Q10 — Open Floor

**"Is there anything about your channel or your growth challenges that we haven't talked about — something you wish a tool like this would solve?"**

*Goal: Catch unmet needs we haven't thought of. Leave space for surprises.*

---

## Post-Interview Notes to Capture

After each session, log the following in `interview_notes.md`:

- **Date / Channel name / Subscriber count / Niche**
- **Thumbnail awareness score (1–5):** Did they know thumbnails were a problem before the audit?
- **Audit reaction (positive / mixed / negative):** What landed?
- **Willingness to pay:** Did they name a number? What did they say about ₹499?
- **Top feature request:** What would have made the audit more useful?
- **Referral signal:** Did they mention other creators / groups?
- **Surprise quote:** Anything they said that we didn't expect?

## Language Variants

### Hindi Script (core questions adapted)

1. *"Aapka channel kab shuru kiya aur kyon? Kya yeh full-time hai ya alongside kuch aur?"*
2. *"Channel grow karne mein sabse mushkil kya lagta hai abhi?"*
3. *"Pehle se thumbnail ke baare mein kitna socha tha? Koi process tha ya bas ban jaata tha?"*
4. *"Audit padh ke kaisa laga? Kuch surprising tha?"*
5. *"Kya audit ke baad kuch change kiya thumbnails mein? Agar nahi, toh kya rok raha hai?"*
6. *"Agar har mahine 3–5 videos ka audit milta rahe, toh aapke liye yeh kitne ka hona chahiye?"*
7. *"₹499 per month reasonable lagta hai? Bahut zyada? Bahut kam?"*
8. *"Iske alawa aur kya helpful hota — video walkthrough, redesigned thumbnail, competitor comparison?"*
9. *"Aap kisi aur creator ko jaante hain jo same problem face kar rahe hain? Share kar sakte hain?"*
10. *"Kuch aur baat karna chahte hain jo hum miss kar gaye?"*

---

### Hinglish Script (mixed — most natural for metro creators)

1. *"Channel kitne time se run kar rahe ho aur kyon start kiya? Side project hai ya full-time?"*
2. *"Growth ke liye sabse bada frustration kya hai abhi?"*
3. *"Audit se pehle thumbnails ke baare mein kitna think kiya tha?"*
4. *"Audit padhke what was your reaction? Kuch surprised kiya?"*
5. *"Koi changes kiye thumbnails mein already? Kya block kar raha hai?"*
6. *"Monthly audit service ke liye kitna pay karna feel right lagega?"*
7. *"₹499/month — fair lagta hai?"*
8. *"Aur kya features helpful hote — video, template, redesign?"*
9. *"Koi aur creator hai jo same problem mein hai? WhatsApp/Telegram group mein share karoge?"*
10. *"Kuch aur jo address nahi hua?"*

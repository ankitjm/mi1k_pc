## WhatsApp Follow-Up Template (Day 3 — After Audit Delivery)

> **Trigger:** Send 3 days after the thumbnail audit has been delivered to the creator. This is a post-delivery follow-up, not a cold outreach. Keep it short, warm, and conversational. Hindi phrases are intentional — they signal that we understand their world.

---

**Message:**

Hey [First Name] 👋

Audit bheja tha 3 din pehle — hope you got a chance to look through it!

Koi suggestion useful lagi? Ya koi cheez confusing lagi toh pooch sakte ho — hum guide kar denge.

If you want, we can hop on a quick 15-min call bhi and walk you through the changes step by step.

— Team ThumbnailOS
thumbnailos.in

---

**Notes for WhatsApp sending:**
- Send from a verified Indian number (saves as a local contact in their phone = higher open rate)
- Do NOT use a business account blast — send as a personal WhatsApp message from the team number
- Keep read receipts on — if they read and don't reply within 24 hours, no further follow-up needed (respect the signal)
- Only send to creators who shared their WhatsApp number on their channel About page or replied to email — never scrape numbers
- If they reply with any question, escalate to CEO within 1 hour — this is a hot lead

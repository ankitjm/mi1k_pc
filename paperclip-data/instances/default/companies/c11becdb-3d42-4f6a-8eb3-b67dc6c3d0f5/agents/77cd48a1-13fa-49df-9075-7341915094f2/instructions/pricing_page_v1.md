## FAQ

**Q: What exactly is in the thumbnail audit?**

The audit looks at your thumbnail across 6 factors: contrast, text readability, face/emotion visibility, clutter score, niche relevance, and mobile preview size. You get a score for each factor plus a short explanation of what to fix and why. It's the same kind of analysis a professional YouTube consultant would give you — automated and available in minutes.

**Q: How does billing work? Will I be charged automatically?**

Yes — Starter and Pro are monthly subscriptions billed automatically on the same date each month via Razorpay. You'll get an email receipt before every charge. You can cancel anytime from your dashboard and you won't be billed again. We don't hide the cancel button.

**Q: I want to cancel. How do I do it?**

Log in → Account Settings → Subscription → Cancel. That's it. No forms, no phone calls, no "please talk to retention first." Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, so you keep access until then.

**Q: Do you support Hindi? My viewers and I are more comfortable in Hindi.**

Yes. Audit PDFs are available in both English and Hindi. WhatsApp alerts (Pro plan) are sent in Hindi by default. If you email us in Hindi, we reply in Hindi. We're building for Indian creators — language shouldn't be a barrier.

**Q: How fast do I get my audit after signing up?**

Free audits are delivered within 2 hours of sign-up (usually faster). Weekly reports for Starter and Pro are delivered every Monday by 9 AM IST. If you sign up mid-week, you'll get a partial report within 24 hours of joining so you're not waiting a full week for your first data.

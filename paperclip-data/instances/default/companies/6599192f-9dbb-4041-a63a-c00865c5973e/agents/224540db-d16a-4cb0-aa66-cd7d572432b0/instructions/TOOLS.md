## Tools & Stack

### Scheduling & Publishing
1. **Sprout Social** — Full-suite platform: scheduling, analytics, social listening, unified inbox, CRM integration; best for mid-to-large ecommerce brands proving social ROI.
2. **Buffer** — Lightweight scheduling and basic analytics; ideal for simpler workflows and quick content queues.
3. **Later** — Visual-first drag-and-drop calendar; Linkin.bio turns the Instagram feed into a shoppable storefront with per-post product links.
4. **Hootsuite** — Enterprise scheduling, team collaboration, bulk publishing, and brand monitoring across 20+ networks.
5. **Agorapulse** — Best-in-class social inbox and community management; unified thread view for comments, DMs, and mentions across platforms.

### Analytics & Reporting
6. **Metricool** — Cross-platform analytics dashboard with competitor benchmarking and best-time-to-post insights.
7. **Google Analytics 4** — Tracks social traffic, assisted conversions, and revenue attribution from social channels.
8. **Socialinsider** — Deep-dive competitive benchmarking and content performance audits across Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Facebook.

### Content Creation
9. **Canva** — Social graphics, carousels, Story templates, and brand kit management; integrates directly with scheduling tools.
10. **CapCut** — Short-form video editing for TikTok/Reels; AI auto-captions, sound sync, and trending effect library.

### Social Listening
11. **Brandwatch** — Enterprise-grade social listening, consumer intelligence, and crisis monitoring with real-time alerts.
12. **Meltwater** — Social listening + influencer discovery and end-to-end campaign management.

### Influencer Marketing
13. **GRIN** — eCommerce-native creator management: AI-powered discovery, product seeding workflow, and ROI tracking integrated with Shopify.
14. **Upfluence** — Influencer discovery with native eCommerce integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce); matches creators by audience affinity to product.

### AI & Workflow
15. **Claude / ChatGPT** — AI-assisted caption drafting, content ideation, repurposing long-form content into social snippets, and A/B test variant generation.

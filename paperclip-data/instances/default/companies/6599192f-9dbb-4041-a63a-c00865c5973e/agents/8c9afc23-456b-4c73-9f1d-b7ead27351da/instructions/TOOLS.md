## Content Tools

### SEO & Research
- **SEMrush** — Full-suite SEO: keyword research, position tracking, site audit, cannibalization report, AI Visibility Toolkit
- **Ahrefs** — Backlink analysis, content gap, keyword research, content explorer
- **Google Search Console** — First-party performance data; use Query Groups for pillar-cluster mapping
- **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** — Traffic, engaged sessions, content-assisted conversions, data-driven attribution
- **Surfer SEO** — On-page content scoring; use at SEO Gate in AI content workflow
- **AlsoAsked** — People Also Ask mapping for FAQ content development on PDPs and category pages
- **SparkToro** — Audience research: where your customers read, watch, listen
- **Keyword Insights** — Keyword clustering, intent grouping, brief generation at scale
- **Screaming Frog** — Technical SEO crawl for content audits; integrates with OpenAI API for bulk alt text

### AI Production & Automation
- **Claude / ChatGPT** — Drafting, ideation, brief generation, FAQ content, meta descriptions
- **Jasper AI** — Brand voice training; content at scale with brand consistency
- **Copy.ai** — E-commerce copy: PDPs, ad copy, email sequences
- **AirOps** — AI workflow automation: bulk PDP refreshes, meta generation, content scaling
- **Grammarly Business** — Quality control, tone enforcement, style guide compliance

### Editorial Planning
- **Airtable** — Editorial calendar, content database, programmatic SEO data management
- **Notion** — Content briefs, SOPs, knowledge wiki, style guide
- **Asana / ClickUp** — Editorial workflow, task management, approvals
- **Planable** — Visual multi-channel content calendar; real-time approval workflows

### CMS & Publishing
- **Shopify** — Primary e-commerce CMS; native AI builder integrations
- **WordPress + WooCommerce** — Flexible content + commerce; WP All Import for programmatic SEO
- **Webflow** — Content-heavy editorial builds without dev dependency

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Looker Studio** — Custom dashboards blending GA4 + GSC
- **Hotjar / Microsoft Clarity** — Heatmaps, scroll depth, session recordings on content pages

### Distribution & Social
- **Later / Buffer** — Social media scheduling across Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest
- **Planable** — Multi-channel visual scheduling with approval workflow
- **Canva for Teams** — Design assets, social graphics, brand kit governance

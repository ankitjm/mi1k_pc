## Principles

1. **Brand clarity over cleverness** — the brand should be instantly understood by someone walking past the stall
2. **Authentic and local** — lean into the street food culture, not corporate polish
3. **Consistency** — every touchpoint (signage, Instagram, packaging) should feel like the same brand
4. **Appetite appeal** — food marketing lives and dies on visuals; prioritize making food look delicious
5. **Start small, build momentum** — launch with a tight content plan rather than over-promising and under-delivering

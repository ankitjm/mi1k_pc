## Netra AI / Khosha Systems — Marketing Scope

**Netra AI marketing is OpenClaw-only.**
All community building content, campaigns, and marketing assets for Netra AI must focus exclusively on OpenClaw / Claw / Paperclip AI agent installation. Do NOT include RetailerOS, Website/SEO packages, IT Support, or other Khosha Systems services in Netra AI content.

- **Why:** Board explicitly corrected this on 2026-04-06. These are separate products/businesses. Netra AI = OpenClaw installation service only.
- **How to apply:** Before writing any Netra AI marketing copy (Marketplace listings, group posts, campaigns, Reels scripts), verify the service offering is strictly OpenClaw/Claw/Paperclip AI. If a brief is ambiguous, ask before writing.
